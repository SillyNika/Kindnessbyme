Return

Changing is very hard. It takes lots of effort and time; it also depends on what you have committed. I said changing is hard, but it's possible and very necessary.
"But what if I don't want to change, Wade?"
Of course, some just feel like they don't need to change. Because why? Why even do it? What's the point?
Almost everything is the point; your life will change a LOT, and I believe in a good way. You will feel much more in life. But more importantly, you changing will help others around you. Don't focus on "I experienced this and I'm sad." Yes, you need comfort, and you will achieve it.
And during the process, instead of thinking:
I experienced this and I'm sad.
think about this:
I experienced this, but there is no need for others to experience it also.
I believe anyone can do it; always put others before yourself and try to guide them using what you learned from your pain and experience.
Because of you, many can be saved or helped. Always remember that in your mind.
