Changing itself is very hard to do,
and not many people wish to change. But changing, in my opinion,
is very, very important. However, there are still times where a person just wishes to not change and simply wants to rest.
I completely understand that, and I believe rest should be given.
But after resting, a person must change, even if it's slow and takes time.
"But Wade, why change? Why can't I remain in my hole forever?"
 You can stay in the hole, but loneliness may come. But more importantly, you need to say to yourself that
there are people who were like you or who were (or are) going through the same stuff as you, and therefore you should use your pain and learn from what happened and help someone who's in need.
Do you want someone else to go through something that you went through? I hope the answer is no.
Also, changing is necessary if you think you went through some bad stuff or if you think you do need help with something. (Find out more about this in the "Do I need to change?" paragraph.)
