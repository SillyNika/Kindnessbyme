hidden truth

You may ask:
Wade, what's this?
It's something that may not fully be connected to my website, but it's still to give people hope. Basically, it's about My story.
I believe not many people have good stories (by stories, I mean the backstory of people's lives). The same goes for me. Sure, I wish to help people, BUT this doesn't mean I haven't gone through pain or that kind of stuff. I have suffered like any human did here and there, but that doesn't mean I should stop doing what I'm supposed to do. And I still believe that because of my backstory, I'm not deserving to be called a
Good human.
Anyways, to put it shortly, I wish to tell you about my backstory.
"Okay Wade, we get it, what's your backstory?" Of course, due to private reasons, I won't tell everything here, but I still want to say most of it. My backstory goes something like this: When I was little, I don't remember lots of stuff. I mostly spent my time around my friends in the place where I live; we had lots of fun here and there, but to put it shortly, I don't remember lots of stuff. Besides all that, when I was about 11-12, I sadly had a very HUGE problem. I kept overthinking and overthinking about lots of stuff. "Did that need to happen?" "Why did that happen?" "Why didn't I do anything?" "Why am I so weak?"
Most of the time it was nothing serious, but I kept overthinking and overthinking, and even if it was a simple joke, I just didn't really want to talk to specific people anymore because every day for 2-3 years just felt so painful for me. Every single day, I wanted to change something. But that, of course, wasn't easy. Nothing huge was going on in my life—just pure pain for years.
But that thankfully changed. I decided to get Discord and try to meet new people and new friends to try to just be happy for once properly. Did that work? First attempt, of course not. I failed, and I didn't meet a good person. But later on, I learned from that and realized my mistake (which I wish to not say here), and later on, I met more people who turned out to be normal. I hang out with them a ton; I was starting to feel so happy and a bit normal. But they were simple friends; we weren't too close. I wanted someone to be REALLY close with and actually trust. But sadly, that did not go so well. I sadly, while also making my own mistakes, put trust into lots of people who sadly ruined it for me, and later on the consequences straight up hit, or I did something dumb and the person who was close to my heart left me. Either way, it didn't go well. But I never gave up, and I looked for a proper person; also, at the same time, I was looking for new friends. I met lots of people—some broke my trust, some gained my trust, but most of them left. I lost many people who were close to me, and many friends sadly.
But I gained new ones at the same time. I also gained VERY trusted people. Again, I felt happy, but I also had despair.
But also, meanwhile in this process, I found Christ. Christianity meant a TON to me. I started to read the Bible and research it again and again. I told my friends, "Don't do this, it's a sin!" or I told them, "Seek Christ." I will explain that more later on, but I kind of "abandoned" Christianity in about 2025.
Anyways, in around 2024, like I said: meeting new people, losing people, etc. But in around the start of 2024, I met three very good people. I won't, of course, say their full names, but their names were something like N, M, and T. They were like "the big three." I talked to them almost every day. They gained my trust, and I have contact with two of them. Yes, sadly I don't and can't have contact with one because of what I did. Even so, in 2024 and even later on, they were still my close friends, and they helped me out SO much. They always put a smile on my face.
And also in 2024, January-ish, I met a special person. I thought they were nice, and they slowly gained my trust, but it did not end well. Why? I also asked myself that, but I sadly found out why the hard way. Our DMs were getting screenshotted, and I believed I got "played" with like a toy. Even so, (while growing with Christ) I still forgave her and later on moved on. Also, in January, I stopped swearing.
June 2024—thought I had a very close friend, but I lost her also due to some stuff. We were having LOTS of fun, but it is, again, okay.
July 2024—met a new special person. It was summer; school ended. I thought to myself: "No more school for now! I can spend more time with her!"
But what happened?
The same ending once more, but we had to let it go due to personal reasons. It's okay; it didn't really last long. (I kept repeating that to myself, and it was the truth.)
During summer, I started to read the Bible, the New Testament; I finished the New Testament in September. I absolutely loved it—pure wisdom shown in one book. So I started to get closer to Christ even more. After summer ended, school started, and thankfully the overthinking was slowly stopping. I felt happy; I was improving here and there. But I still had this one problem I was trying to get rid of for years. I thought it was impossible, but I kept telling myself no, there is hope. (It wasn't overthinking; it was something else). Later on, I said to myself:
"Hey, I'm having this problem, but I believe proper love can fix what I'm going through! I only need to find love! That is the answer and my solution to my problems."
And once more—a new special person. And my theory was correct! Love fixed my problem. But this time, the time with that special person felt so different. I actually felt happy, and it actually felt like it was going to last. The special person actually changed my perspective of the world. Even after everything I experienced to this day, this was still one of the most wholesome things I have ever gone through, and I will never forget it.
But even after all this, it still had to end because of, again, another private reason that I can't tell here. But it was okay. I started to learn from everything I have faced, and I started to learn from people here and there.
But at the same time, when I lost that special person, something else was going on. Sadly, there were protests in the capital of the country that I live in. There were protests every day, but the thing is, they weren't regular. People got hurt on screen, and I just felt so sad and depressed that my people were getting hurt. It hit me in a way that I hadn't experienced before. That, combined with losing that person, just sucked. I felt very, very depressed.
But later on, of course, that changed. 2024 ended, and 2025 started. 2025, just like 2024, was a crazy year. Lots of stuff happened here and there.
Around January-May, nothing huge was happening for me; everything was simply going quietly, but I was still making new friends here and there. But in June, I left Christianity-ish. I simply just didn't believe it anymore. I sadly lost something that was dear to my heart, which was my life and my everything. Yes, I lost my life, but I gained a new one, and I am very glad about that also. I was happy that my hope could be used in another way also.
But any special person?
For that time, no. But I thought that also changed. I thought I found a person again, a special person in the summer. In the summer, I also went to the gym and started making my other website; life was going very great. But, of course, things don't last forever. Sadly, due to my mistake, I lost that special person. Yes, it could have been avoided, but I did something very stupid that I wish to not repeat ever again. I did and will learn from my mistakes. Besides all that, nothing huge happened. School ended—September came. In September, I actually thought I had two special people-ish. The first one I met, well, sadly, simply didn't choose me, and that's okay; we are still friends. I do not blame her. The other one lied about something, so I left all that. Besides that, I thought I had a good friend—a very good friend—who sadly blocked me. But that's also okay. September did suck, and school started also, but I started seeing my homies more and life got better—even if it looked like it was going to be a hard month.
In October-November, I met a very good person named Rei. Rei has been by my side for a long time, and he is very special in my heart.
Nothing huge was happening UNTIL December. Something very crazy hit my life like never before—something that seemed great, yet turned out to be disastrous. First, I met this one girl, one very special girl, whom I loved a ton. I showed her more stuff and shared more information about me. I told her what kind of backstory I had, what I sadly was like, and how horrible my full backstory was. She still accepted me, and we became lovers. She seemed almost exactly like me; I even felt she was my soulmate. I loved her a ton. But, sadly, even that changed also. I still lost her when I tried not to. I sadly did most things, but she didn't do that. It hurt; it hurt so bad. Besides her, a very hard and harsh truth hit my thoughts. I was about to give up and just do what my bad thoughts told me to do, but I didn't do that. Because of my brother and a very, very close friend— friend of friends, homie of homies, the "Curse of the Curses." One of the reasons I'm able to write this is because of him; he saved me and changed my whole perspective. Because of him, even when I went through so much dark stuff, he still stayed by my side and helped me out. Now I am happy. Sometimes, even when I miss that girl, even if she hurt me and some stuff simply went too bad, I still wish to live because being able to feel means that I'm a human. However, I will not be stuck in depression, so I got out of it. Now, at the start of 2026, I met a very good person already. She is dear to my heart; she is a very good friend. And not only that, many friends whom I know from 2024-2025 still remained and didn't disappear. All of them have very special places in my heart, and I will never, NEVER forget about them.
This is it for now. I may add more stuff as life goes on, of course. I won't add absolutely everything personal, but I will try my best to add more stuff. This is my story, for now.
